Discover Peru

The name "Discover Peru" was chosen because it reflects a comprehensive guide for exploring tourist spots, culture, and climate information across Peru.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a user-friendly resource for travelers and enthusiasts who want to explore Peru. The site offers climate details, guides to the most important temples, and tips for discovering the country's cultural and natural highlights.

Scenarios

  • How can I find out the current weather in Lima before planning my trip?
  • Which temples are considered must-visit in Peru, and how can I filter them by region or altitude?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #D72638 - Used for headings, buttons, and important highlights.
Secondary Color: #F9F9F9 - Used as main background and cards.
Accent Color: #1B2A41 - Used for text and navigation bar.

Typography

  • Poppins Bold - For all headings (h1, h2, h3, etc).
  • Poppins Regular - For paragraph text and lists.

Google Fonts is used to ensure cross-browser compatibility.
